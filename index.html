Why Free Forever?

We believe that engineering accuracy is a public good. Whether you are designing a high-rise in Muscat or prototyping a machine in a university lab, the barrier to entry shouldn't be a credit card form.

EngCalc.app is built to be the reliable digital companion for engineers. Optimized for mobile site visits, readable in bright sunlight, and completely free for personal, academic, and commercial projects.
